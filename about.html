Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of data scientists and e-commerce experts, our company was born from a simple observation: traditional forecasting methods were failing to keep up with the rapidly evolving e-commerce landscape.

We set out to build a solution that could harness the power of deep learning to provide accurate, real-time forecasting for online retailers of all sizes. What began as a small startup has grown into a global leader in e-commerce intelligence, serving hundreds of clients across six continents.

Our mission remains the same: to democratize access to advanced AI forecasting tools and help e-commerce businesses thrive in an increasingly competitive market.

Our Technology

Deep Learning Models

Our proprietary neural networks are trained on billions of e-commerce transactions to identify patterns invisible to traditional analytics.

Real-time Processing

Our platform processes millions of data points in real-time, allowing for instant adjustments to forecasting models as market conditions change.

Multi-dimensional Analysis

Unlike traditional forecasting that relies on historical sales data alone, our system analyzes over 200 variables including market trends, competitor pricing, and even weather patterns.

Adaptive Learning

Our models continuously learn and improve from new data, becoming more accurate over time and adapting to your specific business patterns and customer behaviors.
